About Sports Zone

Sports zone with cricket, volleyball, football, basketball, and badminton facilities in the heart of Haridwar. We have 5000 square feet of grass area in total, a well-equipped canteen, free wifi, a sitting area, Ro water, music system, and free parking space that costs only 400 RS onwards for an hour. Yes, you read correctly: 400* for an hour of 6 vs. 6 gameplay.
